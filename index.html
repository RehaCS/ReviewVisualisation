Visualizing fraudulent data!

This website will aim to help you to differentiate between benign and fraudulent applications in the Google Play Store! You will find a series of visualizations, which will show you how real and fake applications differ! View the analysis page to see what differences we found between the visualizations. Why not test yourself to see how many you can spot? Explore and enjoy!

Number of Reviews Overtime

Visualizations which show the number of reviews posted over a period of time.
Distribution of Ratings

Visualizations which show the distribution of rating for a particular application.
Difference in length of reviews

Visualizations which show the difference in the length (number of characters) of the reviews within an application
Average Length of Comment

Visualizations which show the average length of comments for benign and fraudulent applications
Word Cloud

Word Cloud visualizations which show the most frequent words used in benign and fraudulent apps
